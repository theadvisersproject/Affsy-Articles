MuseCam, iphone applications made for photographers !

Better than the most applications available in apple app store for photography through the phone. The camera captures vivid and good photos but with a little bit of editing they end up looking even better. Well thankfully there is a really great app for doing that and is called MuseCam.  This app is an extremely powerful but surprisingly easy to use photo editor for the iphone. It has a lot of features including the basic ones like exposure, contrast, highlights and shadow, along with some advanced ones like RGB curves color by color adjustments for hue saturation, luminance and much more !   The application will definitely improve the quality of your photos and it is a must have for anyone who edits photos on the go. Spark, A multi-manageable email client application <3

Spark is not as good as Newton (The $50 application) neither this app has color coded inbox. But this app is pretty great app nonetheless its fast and it automatically categorize your email and let you quickly make a way to inbox. The app offers features such as snoozing emails, swipe your email right to mark it read and left to either pin or delete. You can even change the default swiping features if you don't like them and can set them which are appropriate to you. All in All the app is a great client for emails for iPhone and I definitely recommend Spark to manage your emails on a peak. You can download Spark (FREE): Click here

Cesium, A music application that people love ♥

iPhone is disliked by a lot of people because it tries to do a lot of things all at once and it usually fails miserably. If you are one of the people that simply don't want to use the default music app, you should definitely checkout cesium. Cesium is an amazing alternative application for playing music. If however you only want to see your local music, you can simply turn off show iCloud items in the app settings. It also has some very nice swipe gestures that you find extremely useful. The settings for the app has a lot of customization options that you can play along. To download cesium you've a pay a little fees that is required on it but trust me, it'll worth it.
